StillHere — Trust & Safety Statement

Last updated: [date]

Our promise

StillHere exists to give peace of mind, not to watch or control people.

We are a quiet check-in service designed for people who live alone, their families, and caregivers.
Everything we build follows one simple rule:

You stay in control. We only act when you don’t respond.

1. How StillHere works (plain English)

You choose when to check in.

You tap “I’m OK” to let your contacts know you’re fine.

If you don’t respond, we notify the people you chose.

Your contacts can check on you before anything else happens.

StillHere does not monitor you continuously.

2. Location sharing (your choice, always)

Location sharing is OFF by default.

We never track your location unless you turn it on.

You can turn location sharing on or off at any time, instantly.

If you choose to enable location sharing:

Your location is shared only during emergencies or shifts, depending on your settings.

We show your contacts when your location was last updated.

We do not build long-term location histories by default.

If location sharing is OFF, no location data is shared.

3. Emergency contacts come first

StillHere is designed to be human-first.

If something goes wrong, we notify your emergency contacts first.

If a contact confirms they are checking on you, we pause further alerts.

This helps avoid unnecessary panic or false alarms.

In the current version of StillHere:

We do not automatically call police or ambulance services.

Your contacts decide what to do next.

4. No hidden monitoring

StillHere does not:

secretly track your location

listen to your microphone

record audio or video

monitor your messages or calls

sell or share your data for advertising

We collect only what is needed to make check-ins work.

5. Minimal data, minimal risk

We design StillHere so there is very little sensitive data to misuse.

We store your most recent check-in time.

Location data (if enabled) is short-lived and limited.

We do not keep long-term movement histories by default.

Contacts see only what is necessary to help you.

This protects you even in the unlikely event of a system breach.

6. Clear consent, no surprises

We believe trust comes from clarity.

We explain what happens if you miss a check-in.

We show exactly when alerts are sent.

We show when location sharing is active.

You can pause alerts if you are busy, sleeping, or travelling.

Nothing happens silently.

7. Security & abuse prevention

We actively prevent misuse of StillHere:

Login is protected by one-time verification codes.

Alert sending is rate-limited to prevent spam.

Contact access links are secure and can be revoked.

We monitor for abnormal usage patterns.

Abuse of the service is not tolerated.

8. Who StillHere is for

StillHere is designed for:

people living independently

elderly users who want reassurance without surveillance

caregivers and families

lone workers who need a simple safety check-in

It is not designed for tracking people without consent.

9. Our commitment

If we ever change how StillHere works in a meaningful way:

we will explain it clearly

we will not remove your control

we will not turn on tracking without consent

Trust is not optional — it is the product.
